First topic: War
Assignment 1:

Assignment 2:
  • Walzer, Just and Unjust Wars, Part I (pp. 3-47). For those of you who don't yet have the book, here is a copy of the assigned text.
  • Kilner, "Military Leaders' Obligation to Justify Killing in War." The whole piece is accessible and interesting. For our purposes the most relevant portion is the last section, pp. 29-31, but at least skim the rest of the article.
  • Optional: Thucydides, "The Mytilene Episode" from The Peloponnesian War (e-reserve). Notice that Walzer contrasts the Melian dialogue with the Mytilene episode (pp. 9-12); here is the text he is talking about.

  • Orend ch. 8, “Evaluating the Realist Alternative” from The Morality of War (e-reserve).

Assignment 6:
  • Walzer ch. 16, “Supreme Emergency”
  • Optional: Orend ch. 5, "Jus in Bello #2: Supreme Emergencies" (e-reserves)

  • Anscombe, "War and Murder" and "Mr. Truman's Degree" (e-reserve; both are together in one file).
