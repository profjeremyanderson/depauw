Each week I will create study questions covering the main points for you to remember from the previous week's lecture/discussions and readings. Exams will consist of some of these questions.

The study questions will be posted here for you. I will notify you by e-mail whenever new ones are posted.

I strongly recommend that whenever new questions are posted you
1. review the readings and your lecture notes,
2. write out the clearest, most thorough answers you can to these questions, and
3. study those answers periodically.
Exams will be evaluated by how well you demonstrate you understand, and have thought about, course materials. So make your answers as thorough and clear as you can in the time available -- in other words, show off how much you know.

I encourage you to work together with others in the class on this. Of course you will be responsible for your own answers on exams, though.

If you have trouble coming up with answers, contact me for help. You may discuss your answers with me to improve them.
